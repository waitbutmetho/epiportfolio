About Me

I was born and raised in the state of New Hampshire. I was brought up in a strict household where values like hard work, honesty, and integrity were instilled at a young age. My mother home-schooled me for my early childhood, and most of middle school. I was a three season athlete in high school starting for my basketball team as a guard for all four years. I was also a helpful team member in cross country, track, and field. I graduating from a private school in South Berwick, ME in 2013, achieving honors for two semester and high honors for the other two my senior year.
